What is Rupie?

Rupie is an exciting new gaming community that provides powerful ways for gamers and game developers to make revenue in a fun economy, engage in social venues like never before, and above all, participate in contributing to the ever evolving world where we play games.

We are giving the power back to those who play and make games, come join us!

While increasing developer accountability

Build games together

Together we can fix the distrust associated with early access, while at the same leveraging the immense talent of the community to help forge the games we like playing.

With better community game incubation, game developers get their game funded and gamers vote on whether the next development milestone should include a new map or new weapons.

By setting deliverable milestones, early access developers are able to get partial funding based on meeting certain criteria — essentially escrow tiers. Didn't finish your game? You dont get paid.

About Us

Rent out your quality assets

Got a bunch of 3d models lying around from an old game of yours?

With Asset Rentals, as a developer, youll be able to bind a contract with other developers to use that content you spent weeks on, and give it new life!

Browse through a large list of assets in a rich interface to find that perfect script, model or audio file you have been looking for — and integrate seamlessly with the Rupie Network.

Youll be building games your fellow developers work in a way that is far more collaborative than the existing model.
