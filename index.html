Hey, listen

Rupie enables games to be treated as iterative development projects. Instead of a house that gets planned for, built, finished and sold; we believe that games are adaptive, evolving and subject to contribution by a much wider audience for a potentially indefinite period of time.

Rupie fundamentally redesigned crowdfunding as a safe haven for Developers, Players and Content Creators to come together and materialize game concepts in a symbiotic way.

Now more than ever, players and developers need each other. Over 75% of Early Access and Kickstarter games won't see the light of day (with no respawn, srsly).

Introducing: Community Game Incubation

The Dungeon Heart of Rupie

Say hello to a new convention for unifying players, developers, and content creators that makes developing games inclusive, rewarding and fun. At the center of it all, is a simple three step process that provides incredible value for producing a new era of games.

Fund

Give life to projects you love

Rupie works just like normal crowdfunding, except backers fund projects with tokens that are kept in escrow on the blockchain. Then developers unlock those funds in chunks we call Milestones, as they demonstrate progress to the community.

Build

Where it all comes together

Milestone escrow puts the developer and backers on the same team for the first time, making the game succeed, together. This will drastically increase the success rate of games, and in the worst case, the remaining escrow can be refunded.

Connect

A place for contribution

Rupie comes with powerful community tools baked in.

  • Backers can suggest (even fund!) features that the developer can approve into official releases.
  • Bugs can be filed right from the forum comment thread.
  • And much more...

Asset marketplace

Creative content ftw

With Rupie, content creators are first-class citizens in game development. Post high quality models, audio, and more to the Asset marketplace, where developers will have tools to seamlessly buy and integrate your work, getting you paid.

Content bounty system

Content creators, meet developers

As a developer, it can be difficult to manage your project and find bandwidth (or skill!) to create all the assets. With Rupie Bounties, developers can buy or rent-to-own assets, from the talent right in their community. Content creators get their art in the game and get paid in the process.
